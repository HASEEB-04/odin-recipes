Sweet potato turkey meatloaf is a savory and comforting dish that combines lean ground turkey with the natural sweetness and creamy texture of sweet potatoes. The meatloaf is made with ground turkey, which is mixed with finely chopped onions,
garlic, and a blend of herbs and spices. Mashed sweet potatoes are incorporated into the mixture, adding moisture and a subtle sweetness that contrasts beautifully with the savory turkey. Often, an egg and some
breadcrumbs or oats are added to help bind the ingredients together and ensure a tender texture.The mixture is formed into a loaf shape and baked until itâ€™s cooked through and golden brown on the outside.

Ingredients

  • Large Sweet Potato
  • Ketchup
  • Worcestershire
  • Ground Turkey
  • Egg
  • Onion Flakes
  • Marjoram
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook your potatoes in the microwave
  2. Remove the skin of potatoes and mash the flesh in a large bowl.
  3. Combine all the meatloaf ingredients except Ketchup and Worcestershire
  4. Mix the ketchup and Worcestershire sauce and brush it on top of the meatloaf.
  5. Bake uncovered for 55 minutes at 350F.
  6. Let it sit for five minutes before slicing.

How to Freeze Meatloaf?

Leftover turkey meatloaf will keep in the fridge for three to four days and in the freezer for three months.
Store in a freezer safe container or wrap in foil. You can thaw a serving overnight in the refrigerator and microwave it when ready to eat.
